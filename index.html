Ragdolls

Ragdolls are a sweet and docile breed. Four of my cats are ragdolls and i chose this breed because of the various patterns they come in as well as for their fluffy luxurious coats and big beautiful blue eyes. Did you know Ragdolls can play fetch like dogs?! My cats will carry their favourite toys around and place them by my feet when its fetch time.
